About Us

Our journey began in 2024 when our founder, Mokgabo decided to turn a passion for baking into a business. It all started with her baking for family and friends at home, trying out different flavors, textures, and techniques and towards the end of 2024, the dream of a bakery came to life. Today, we are proud to be working with her, serving Block 6 and neighbouring places with a variety of baked goods made from the finest ingredients.

Our Mission- Baking with heart: At The Pink Whisk Bakery, our mission is to create delicious, high-quality baked goods. We believe in the power of food to bring people together, celebrate moments of joy, and comfort in times of need.Whether it’s a loaf of freshly baked bread, a birthday cake, or just a sweet treat, we are here to make your day a little sweeter.

Our Values

Quality First

We take pride in using only the best ingredients. Every item that leaves our bakery is made with the highest standards of quality and care.

Sustainability

We care about the environment and are actively working towards reducing our impact. We source ingredients responsibly, minimize waste, and use eco-friendly packaging whenever possible.

Customer Happiness

We are passionate about making sure every visit to our bakery is a delightful experience, whether you’re picking up a quick treat or ordering a custom cake for a special occasion. We bake fresh every day with love and care. Come visit us for the best bakery items in town!
